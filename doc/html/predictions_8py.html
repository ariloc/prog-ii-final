Score calc_score ( Probability   unigram = 0.0,
Probability   leftBigram = 0.0,
Probability   rightBigram = 0.0,
Probability   trigram = 0.0 
)

Calculates the Score for a word \(W_j\) to be predicted as a replacement for the missing word \(W_m\) in a trigram \((W_i W_m W_k)\), given the probabilities of the unigram \((W_i)\), the left and right bigrams \((W_i W_j)\) and \((W_j W_k)\) respectively, and the trigram \((W_i W_j W_k)\), in a list of sentences.

This score is calculated based on certain fixed weights \( \lambda_1 = \) WEIGHT_UNIGRAM, \( \lambda_2 = \) WEIGHT_LEFT_BIGRAM, \( \lambda_3 = \) WEIGHT_RIGHT_BIGRAM and \( \lambda_4 = \) WEIGHT_TRIGRAM where \(\lambda_1 + \lambda_2 + \lambda_3 + \lambda_4 = 1\):

\[ \text{Score}(W_j) = \lambda_1 \text{P}(W_j) + \lambda_2 \text{P}(W_j|W_i) + \lambda_3 \text{P}(W_j|W_k) + \lambda_4 \text{P}(W_j|W_i,W_k) \]

May also be used to calculate the score for bigrams or unigrams with the missing probabilities (of the longer n-grams) left as 0.0.

Parameters
unigramThe probability of \((W_j)\) containing a certain word.
leftBigramThe probability of the left bigram \((W_i W_j)\).
rightBigramThe probability of the right bigram \((W_j W_k)\).
trigramThe probability of the trigram \((W_i W_j W_k)\).
Returns
The score for the word \(W_j\) to be predicted as a replacement for the missing word \(W_m\) in the trigram \((W_i W_m W_k)\).
